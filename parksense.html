How it works

From sensor to screen
in under a second

Three steps. Zero hassle. Every time.

01

Sensor detects

An ultrasonic sensor in each spot detects vehicles in real time — no cameras, no moving parts, just a clean instant signal.

02

LoRa transmits

Status is sent instantly over LoRa — long range and ultra-low power. Works underground and in dense concrete structures.

03

You see it live

Open the app, see a live floor map with exact free and taken spots per level. Know before you arrive.

04

Operators gain insight

Live dashboard with occupancy trends, peak hours, and sensor health — all in one portal for facility managers.

Features

Built for real
parking environments

Designed for underground car parks, open lots, hospitals, and malls.

Real-time updates

Status changes reach your phone in under 1 second. Event-driven — only transmits on change, not on a polling cycle.

LoRa long range

One LoRaWAN gateway covers an entire mall or terminal — underground included. No per-spot wiring ever.

3+ year battery life

Designed for minimal maintenance. Event-driven LoRa keeps power consumption near zero at rest.

Modular hardware

Fixed station with a swappable sensor unit. Replace in seconds — no tools, no rewiring, zero downtime.

Operator dashboard

Full analytics — occupancy by hour, peak trends, and live sensor health for management teams.

IP67 weatherproof

Sealed against rain, cleaning jets, and vehicle fluids. Rated for both outdoor and underground use.
